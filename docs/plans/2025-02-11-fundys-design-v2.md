# Fundy's Website - Design v2

**Project:** Single-page showcase website for Fundy's Spreads
**Stack:** Next.js + shadcn/ui + Tailwind CSS
**Theme:** Warm & Cozy, premium artisan, photo-heavy
**Fonts:** Pacifico (headings) + Inter (body text)
**Inspiration:** Beerenberg, Stonewall Kitchen, Wozz Kitchen Creations

---

## Color Palette (Matched to Logo)

```
Primary Background:   #FAF7F2  - Cream/Off-white
Brand Red:            #8B1A1A  - Deep red (from logo)
Brand Red Hover:      #6B1414  - Darker red
Accent:               #C4A484  - Warm brown (label tones)
Text Primary:         #3D3D3D  - Charcoal
Text Light:           #FAF7F2  - Cream (on dark backgrounds)
Footer Background:    #8B1A1A  - Deep red
```

## Typography

| Usage | Font | Weight | Size |
|-------|------|--------|------|

## Page Layout (Single-Page Scrolling)

### 1. Header (Sticky)

- Logo on left
- Nav links on right: Our Spreads | Our Story | Contact
- Semi-transparent cream background
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section (Product-First)

- Full-width cream background
- Centered: dual-jar product photo (both flavors side by side)
- Above photo: "Fundy's" in large Pacifico script (or logo)
- Below photo: "Gourmet Cheese Spread — Made with Imported Edam Cheese"
- CTA button: "Shop on Shopee" in deep red
- No carousel — one strong hero image

### 3. Our Spreads (2-Column Grid + Bundle Banner)

- Section heading: "Our Spreads" in Pacifico
- 2-column grid (centered):
  - Left card: Quezo de Bola Pimiento
  - Right card: Truffle de Bola
- Each card:
  - Large product photo (single-jar shot)
  - Product name in script
  - "Gourmet Cheese Spread" subtitle
  - Price placeholder
  - "Shop on Shopee" button
- Below cards: Full-width banner with dual-jar photo + "Shop All on Shopee" CTA
- Mobile: Cards stack vertically

### 4. Our Story (About)

- Warm cream background
- Side-by-side layout:
  - Left: Behind-the-scenes / lifestyle photo
  - Right: 2-3 paragraphs about Fundy's story
- Placeholder text until content is gathered
- Mobile: Stacked vertically

### 5. Footer (Deep Red)

- Background: Deep red (#8B1A1A)
- Text: Cream
- Social links with icons: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Shopee
- Contact info (email, phone)
- Copyright: "2025 Fundy's Spreads"
